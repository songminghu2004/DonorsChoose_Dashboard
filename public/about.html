TENDRTaking Education National Donations Rural
The TENDR website is based off of the DonorsChoose.org open data. For this website we used the project data set. This data set was imported into a MongoDB and was connected to the web front end throug NodeJS. The websites goal is to bring donors and teachers closer together. From the teacher's side it allows them to understand which types of projects get funding. From the Donor's side they can see requests in their region or a region they care about and more easily discover schools in need that match they giving profile.

The initial intent was to allow rural donations to catch up with urban as there is a huge disparity between the two types of projects. This did morph to be more broadly reaching as we went through the data discovery and exploration process. Donations are like all business, you need a provider and a consumer, they need to understand eachother and this site helps to bridge that divide.

Goals:
  • Bring the donor closer to the teacher and vice-versa.
  • Allow an easy way to see where donations are being requested and tie that to where the donations are coming from
  • Drive the ability for a teacher to see which types of request have the best chance of being funded

    Audience:
  • Primarily Teachers
  • Secondarily Donors -- this actually came with going through the exploration and realizing it is valuable for donors as well

    Data Sources:
  • DonorsChoose.org open data
